About BB84 Protocol
Quantum Bit Generation

Alice generates random bits and encodes them using randomly chosen bases (rectilinear + or diagonal ×).

Quantum Channel

Qubits are transmitted through a quantum channel. Any eavesdropping disturbs the quantum states.

Measurement

Bob measures received qubits using randomly chosen bases, keeping only bits where bases match.

Key Sifting

Alice and Bob compare bases publicly and discard mismatched measurements, creating a sifted key.

Error Estimation

A subset of the key is compared to estimate quantum bit error rate (QBER). High QBER indicates eavesdropping.

Privacy Amplification

The final key is shortened using hash functions to eliminate any information an eavesdropper might have.
